Some rules of the Quiz

  1. Once you click on Start Quiz button, the timer starts.
  2. You can only start the Quiz only after you have entered your name.
  3. Once you select your answer, it can't be undone.
  4. You can't go to the previous questions once you have answered it.
  5. The quiz can only end if the timer is complete or you have answered all questions.
  6. You'll get points on the basis of your correct answers.
